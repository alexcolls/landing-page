Here in Beenzer we created

an app for you to interact with friends and share experiences with them. Be Part of this too!

How it works?

You download the app, connect with your Phantom wallet and travel or go for a walk, post a picture in the Map inside the app and when your friends are in the same area where you got the pictures posted they will be able to see those pics! But what makes Beenzer different is the fact that you can transform your photos into a NFT, so your memories are registred as unique, also you can sell your NTF's and buy from your friends!
